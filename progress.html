Progress

Basic usage

75%
<div class="progress" role="progressbar">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 75%;">75%</div>
</div>

Colors

replace {color} with the color name (primary, secondary, red, ...etc)
<div class="progress {color}" role="progressbar">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 75%;">75%</div>
</div>

Sizes

replace {size} with the size (xs, sm, lg, xl)
<div class="progress {size}" role="progressbar">
    <div class="progress-bar" style="width: 75%;">75%</div>
</div>
